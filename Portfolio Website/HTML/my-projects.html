    Online Nagar Panchayat

    2023

    Online Nagar Panchayat is a project sponsored by Guhagar Nagar Panchayat. The office of Nagar Panchayat is located on the top floor of Bank of India in Guhagar. Through this website we are trying to provide maximum convenience to the people living within the limits of the Nagar Panchayat. With the help of this website, people can pay taxes online from homes like homestead, water strip. Also, people can get various types of certificates online like resident certificates, below poverty line certificates, marriage certificates as well as birth and death certificates and so on.

    Furniture Management System

    2022

    This project is about the Furniture Shop Management System. Furniture is the soul of a house; it gives a sense of fulfilling needs. Types of furniture are of many designs and categories. It can be as small as a mouse to as gigantic as an elephant. Effective management of a Furniture Shop System ensures that customers can effortlessly browse through an extensive range of furniture, from elegant chairs to functional storage solutions. Efficient management accommodates all customer needs, from small to large, ensuring a seamless shopping experience.
